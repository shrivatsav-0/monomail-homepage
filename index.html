What's new

v1.2.5
Smart Grouping

Frequent senders like Reddit and GitHub are automatically collapsed into expandable folders, keeping your inbox focused.

Redesigned Settings

Grouped card-based sections replace the flat list — cleaner visual hierarchy, easier to navigate.

Live Font Size Preview

See real-time text scaling in Settings before you apply — no surprises after tapping confirm.

M3 Modal Bottom Sheets

All settings pickers now use Material 3 bottom sheets instead of dropdowns — proper mobile-native feel.

Spring Physics Modals

Profile, account switch, and add account modals now scale in from centre with spring physics — no more slide-from-edge.

Blur Timing Fixes

80ms blur delay eliminates the one-frame background flash when opening modals. Every transition now feels intentional.

Why Monomail

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Offline-First

Built on Room Database. Changes apply instantly and sync with Gmail in the background — works without a connection.

Background Sync

WorkManager keeps your inbox up to date. Notifications and status changes happen flawlessly behind the scenes.

Offline + Online Search

Fully integrated search that works offline and online, woven into navigation with a morphing SearchBar.

Smart Grouping

High-frequency senders are automatically collapsed into expandable folders with animated spring expand/collapse.

Swipe Gestures

Swipe right to Archive / Unarchive. Swipe left to Star / Unstar. Fluid, native-feeling, and configurable from Settings.

Smart Undo

Accidentally archived or deleted? A morphing toast gives you 4 seconds to undo — without firing a network request.

Redesigned Settings

Grouped card-based sections, M3 Modal Bottom Sheet pickers, and live font size preview before you apply changes.

Long Press Menu

Hold any email to Star, Archive, toggle Read/Unread, or Delete — in a clean contextual card with smooth animations.

Compose & Reply

Distraction-free compose with attachment support, confirm-before-send, and configurable reply defaults.

Multiple Accounts

Add Gmail and Microsoft Outlook accounts and switch between them effortlessly. All your inboxes, one app.

Unified Inbox

See all your accounts in one merged feed. One scroll, every email — no context switching.
